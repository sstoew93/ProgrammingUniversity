Close Encounters

Close encounters with aliens and abductions are common themes in ufology, blending anecdotal experiences, folklore, and modern popular culture. "Close encounters" categorize sightings and experiences with UFOs or extraterrestrial beings. These were formalized by astronomer J. Allen Hynek:

  • CE-1 (Close Encounters of the First Kind): Sighting of a UFO within 500 feet, showing detail but without physical interaction.
  • CE-2 (Second Kind): A UFO leaves physical evidence, such as imprints on the ground, crop circles, or radiation.
  • CE-3 (Third Kind): Encounter with animated beings, often described as extraterrestrial.
  • Extended Categories: Later interpretations added "CE-4" (abduction by aliens).

This app is for enthusiasts who want to share their stories.
